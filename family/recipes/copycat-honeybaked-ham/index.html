Copycat Honey Baked Ham

Ingredients

Tap to check off as you go
  • 8 lb bone-in spiral sliced half ham
  • 2 Tbsp butter, melted
  • 3 Tbsp honey
  • Glaze (dry rub)
  • 1½ cups granulated sugar
  • ½ tsp seasoned salt
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves
  • ¼ tsp paprika
  • pinch of allspice

Method

  1. Trim ham to fit slow cooker if needed. Pat dry with paper towels (the bag liquid will make the butter slide right off otherwise).
  2. Combine melted butter + honey. Massage over ham, getting some between the slices.
  3. Cover and cook on **LOW for 4-5 hours**.
  4. Preheat broiler to HIGH. Line a roasting pan with heavy-duty foil.
  5. Combine sugar, seasoned salt, onion powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, cloves, paprika, allspice in a small bowl.
  6. Transfer ham to roasting pan. Pat **half** of the sugar mixture over the top.
  7. Broil a few minutes until bubbly and caramelized. Remove from oven.
  8. In a small saucepan, add the **remaining** sugar mixture + 3 Tbsp water OR ham juices from the slow cooker (bourbon swap works too). Stir, bring to a boil, boil ~1 min, remove from heat.
  9. Brush/pour glaze over the ham. Broil **1-2 more minutes** — watch it, sugar burns fast.
  10. Rest 5-10 min at room temp so the glaze hardens into the crackly crust.

Notes & pitfalls

Notes / pitfalls

  • DRY the ham before the butter/honey rub or it slides off.
  • Two broil passes, not one. First pass sets the crust, second pass glazes it.
  • Don't walk away during broiling. Carbonized sugar = sad ham.
  • Bourbon in the saucepan instead of plain water adds depth.
