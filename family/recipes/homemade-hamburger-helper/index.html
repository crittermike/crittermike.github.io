Homemade Hamburger Helper

Ingredients

Tap to check off as you go
  • 2 lb ground beef
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced (optional but obvious)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream (or whole milk in a pinch)
  • 1 lb large elbow macaroni (the chunky kind)
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Brown beef + onion in a large deep skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high. Drain fat if it's swimming.
  2. Stir in garlic, tomato paste, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, pepper. Cook 1 min until tomato paste darkens.
  3. Pour in beef broth + cream. Scrape the bottom. Bring to a boil.
  4. Add elbows. Stir. Cover, reduce to medium-low, simmer 10-12 min until pasta is tender and sauce thickens. Stir a couple times so the bottom doesn't catch.
  5. Kill the heat. Stir in shredded cheddar until melted and glossy.
  6. Salt to taste. Rest 2-3 min so it sets up.

Notes & pitfalls

Notes

  • Want it sharper? Use sharp cheddar or half cheddar / half Monterey jack.
  • Thinning out? Splash more broth or milk.
  • Too thin? Uncover the last few minutes.
  • Mike protein target: skip a half-portion of pasta, add a side of cottage cheese or boiled eggs to hit ~62g.
