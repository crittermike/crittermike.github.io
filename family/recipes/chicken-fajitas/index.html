Chicken Fajitas (Cast Iron)

Protein
48g
Calories
600
Kids
Yes

Ingredients

Tap to check off as you go
  • 1.5–2 lb chicken breasts, sliced into 1/4" strips
  • 2 tbsp fajita seasoning (or to taste)
  • 2 medium onions, sliced into half-moons
  • 2 medium zucchini, halved lengthwise then sliced 1/4" thick
  • 1.5 cups corn (frozen or fresh kernels off the cob)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 8–10 tortillas
  • Salt + pepper

Method

  1. **Marinate:** Toss sliced chicken with 1 tbsp olive oil and 1.5 tbsp fajita seasoning. Let it sit 15+ min while you prep veg.
  2. **Heat the cast iron:** Crank a 12" cast iron over medium-high until it's smoking lightly (~3 min).
  3. **Sear chicken:** Add chicken in a single layer, don't crowd. Cook untouched 2–3 min until charred, flip and cook another 2 min. Pull to a plate. Work in two batches if needed.
  4. **Cook onions:** Add remaining 1 tbsp oil and onions to the same pan. 3–4 min until they get color.
  5. **Add zucchini + corn:** Toss in zucchini and corn with the rest of the fajita seasoning + pinch of salt. Cook 4–5 min, stirring once or twice. You want char on the zucchini, not mush. Don't stir constantly.
  6. **Combine:** Return chicken (and any juices) to the pan, toss everything together 1 min to reheat.
  7. **Warm tortillas:** Wrap in foil and stick in a 300°F oven for 5 min, or char them one at a time over an open gas flame for 10 sec/side.
  8. **Serve:** Pile into tortillas. Toppings optional but lime juice, cilantro, salsa, or a dab of sour cream all work.

Notes & pitfalls

Pitfalls

  • Don't crowd the pan or chicken steams instead of searing.
  • Zucchini turns to mush fast — keep it moving and pull it before it looks done.
  • Cast iron stays scorching after you turn it off, so move stuff off the heat once chicken is back in.
