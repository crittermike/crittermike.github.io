Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Serves
6
Prep
30 min
Cook
20 min
Protein
30g
Calories
471
Kids
Yes

Ingredients

Tap to check off as you go
  • Chicken
  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts (~1 lb)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • Pasta + Sauce
  • 8 oz rigatoni, cooked al dente
  • 4 tbsp (½ stick) unsalted butter
  • ¼ cup shallot, finely diced
  • ¼ cup sun-dried tomatoes, softened in hot water and chopped
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp kosher salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1½ cups heavy cream
  • 1½ cups milk
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated (plus more for garnish)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish
  • Salt + pepper, to taste

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Drizzle 2 tbsp olive oil over the chicken on all sides, rub it in.
  2. Combine paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Sprinkle over chicken on all sides.
  3. In a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high, heat the remaining 1 tbsp oil. Sear chicken 3 min per side until browned (not cooked through).
  4. Transfer skillet to oven, cook 10–15 min until internal temp hits 165°F. Move chicken to a plate, tent to keep warm. ### Pasta + Sauce
  5. Boil rigatoni in salted water, cook al dente (~14 min). Drain.
  6. In the same skillet, melt butter over medium. Add shallots + sun-dried tomatoes, cook 3–5 min until softened. Add garlic, cook 1 more min.
  7. Add tomato paste, oregano, paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper. Stir.
  8. Add heavy cream and milk, stir to combine.
  9. Add spinach, parmesan, and cherry tomatoes. Reduce heat to low, simmer 8–10 min until cheese melts and spinach wilts.
  10. Add cooked pasta, toss to coat. Slice or cube the chicken and fold in.
  11. Serve topped with extra parmesan and parsley. Salt + pepper to taste.

Notes & pitfalls

Pitfalls

  • Chicken won't be cooked through after searing — the oven finishes it. Pull at 165°F so it doesn't dry out.
  • Soften the sun-dried tomatoes in hot water first or they stay leathery.
  • Sauce thickens as it sits, so don't over-reduce before adding pasta.
  • Nancy-safe: no mushrooms, peppers, fish, feta, lemon, or cilantro. Clear.
