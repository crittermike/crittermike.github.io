White Chicken Chili

Serves: 6 Prep: 15 min Cook: 4 hr (crockpot, low) Total: 4 hr 15 min

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (15 oz) great northern beans, drained & rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) cannellini beans, drained & rinsed
  • 2 cans (4 oz) diced green chiles
  • 1 can (15 oz) corn, drained
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 cup heavy cream or half & half
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack
  • Toppings: cilantro, lime wedges, tortilla strips, sour cream, avocado

Instructions

  1. Add chicken, onion, garlic, beans, chiles, corn, broth, and spices to the crockpot. Stir.
  2. Cover and cook on LOW for 4 hours (or HIGH for 2 hours) until chicken is cooked through.
  3. Remove chicken, shred with two forks, return to pot.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and shredded cheese. Cook on LOW another 15 min until creamy.
  5. Taste and adjust salt. Serve with toppings and cornbread on the side.

Notes

  • For thicker chili, mash one can of beans before adding.
  • Henry hates cilantro — keep it on the side.
  • Freezes great. Double batch and freeze half.
