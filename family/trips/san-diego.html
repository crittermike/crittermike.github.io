🏛️ About the Venue

The Junipero Serra Museum is a stunning historic landmark in Presidio Park, perched on a hilltop overlooking Mission Valley and the San Diego River. Built in 1929 in Spanish Colonial Revival style, the museum marks the site where Father Junipero Serra established the first Spanish mission in California in 1769.

🌅 Why it's special: Gorgeous panoramic views of San Diego, beautiful Spanish architecture, lush gardens, and a sense of history that makes for an unforgettable wedding backdrop. The hilltop setting means golden hour photos will be incredible.

🌴 While We're There

San Diego has tons to offer if you want to explore beyond the wedding:

  • 🏛️
    Marston House
    3525 7th Ave, San Diego, CA 92103 · (619) 297-9327
    Historic Arts & Crafts mansion from 1905.
  • ⚓
    Embarcadero
    1492 N Harbor Dr, San Diego, CA 92101
    Cruise-ship hub with Seaport Village (seafood, souvenir shops), The Headquarters (chic eateries, boutiques in an old police command post), USS Midway Museum (former aircraft carrier with flight simulators and restored planes), Waterfront Park with ocean views.
  • 🌿
    Balboa Park
    San Diego, CA · (619) 239-0512
    Cultural oasis with 18 museums, beautiful gardens, and the world-famous San Diego Zoo. 1,200 acres, one of North America's most renowned urban parks. Must-see.
  • 🏘️
    Heritage Park
    2454 Heritage Park Row, San Diego, CA 92110
    Victorian gem in Old Town, beautifully restored Victorian-era homes from the 1880s-1890s.
  • 🏄
    Windansea Park
    6600 Neptune Pl, La Jolla, CA 92037 · (619) 221-8899
    Picturesque surfing spot in La Jolla with scenic sandstone points and a historic palm-covered surf shack.
